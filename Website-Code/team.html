Meet the Team

The ones who get things done...

Rithvan Senthiil

Team Leader / Main Programmer

Read Bio

Hi, I’m Rithvan Senthiil. Over the past few years, my interest in the STEM field has grown. Robotics and Science Fairs have been two of the largest STEM activities I’ve participated in. I have 3 years of experience in FIRST LEGO League (FLL), where I qualified for the Ohio State Championship twice and gained hands-on skills in robotics, programming logic, and teamwork. I’ve also competed in science fairs for 5 years, advancing to the Ohio State Science Fair twice with aerospace engineering projects involving electronics, programming, and 3D modeling. I presented one of these projects at a NASA convention center and to Boeing engineers, collaborating with Dhruv Prasanna to showcase our work. I’ve also been part of my school’s Mathcounts team for two years and built several small projects using Python and HTML.

Dhruv Prasanna

Main Programmer / Electronics / CAD

Read Bio

Hi, I'm Dhruv Prasanna, and my passion for STEM has grown steadily over the years. I've spent 6 years competing in FIRST LEGO League (FLL), where I qualified for the Ohio State Championship twice and developed hands-on skills in robotics, programming, and teamwork. I've also participated in science fairs for 5+ years, advancing to the Ohio State Science Fair twice, with award-winning projects focused on rockets, propulsion systems, and airflow dynamics. My work has led me to present at a NASA convention center and to Boeing engineers, where I received valuable feedback and gained exposure to professional aerospace research. Along the way, I've learned programming languages like C++, Java, HTML, and Python, and continue to explore the intersection of creativity and technical precision in everything I do.
