Outreach

Get to know what we provide to the community!

Article Hub

Article Hub is a page in our website where we have useful articles that include tips and tricks for other FTC teams. As a team that has gone through 1 full season of FTC, we decided to share our knowledge gained from that season so that other teams won't have a rough start. We will contantly update the hub with new articles that provide useful information for teams!

FLL Team Mentorship

One of our proudest accomplishments is mentoring a rookie FIRST LEGO League team. Having spent five years in FLL ourselves, we understand the challenges, excitement, and learning opportunities that come with those crucial first seasons.

We provide hands-on guidance in robot design, programming fundamentals, project research, and core values development. Our mentorship goes beyond just the technical aspects – we help young teams develop confidence, collaboration skills, and a lifelong passion for STEM.

Weekly mentorship sessions during build season Competition day coaching and support Shared resources and lesson plans
