Outreach

Get to know what we provide to the community!

Article Hub

Article Hub is a page in our website where we have useful articles that include tips and tricks for other FTC teams. As a team that has gone through 1 full season of FTC, we decided to share our knowledge gained from that season so that other teams won't have a rough start. We will constantly update the hub with new articles that provide useful information for teams! Click the link above to get there or head over to the resources dropdown in the menu!

FLL Team Mentorship

One of our proudest accomplishments is mentoring a rookie FIRST LEGO League team. Having spent five years in FLL ourselves, we understand the challenges, excitement, and learning opportunities that come with those crucial first seasons.

We provide hands-on guidance in robot design, programming fundamentals, project research, and core values development. Our mentorship goes beyond just teh technical aspects. We help young teams develop confidence, collaboration skills, and an interest for STEM.

----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

The past year, we came to a majority of the FLL team meetings and spent time teaching and mentoring students. This experience has provided younger generations with more knowledge going forward and has provided benefits such as confidence and leadership skills for us. We would like to continue this in the future and if your FLL team would like a mentor please don't hesitate to contact us!
