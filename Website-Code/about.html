About Us

Our Origin

The Emerald Engineers didn't start in FTC – our roots run deep in the FIRST community. For five transformative years, we honed our skills in FIRST LEGO League, where we learned the fundamentals of robotics, teamwork, and problem-solving that would define our approach to engineering. Those FLL seasons weren't just about building robots; they were about building ourselves. We learned to think critically under pressure, collaborate effectively across different personalities and skillsets, and tackle real-world challenges with both technical precision and creative flair. Every season brought new lessons, new friendships, and new perspectives on what it means to be part of the FIRST community.

Transitioning to FTC

As we grew older and our passion for robotics intensified, the natural next step was FIRST Tech Challenge. While we're technically a "rookie" FTC team, we bring years of experience, countless hours of hands-on learning, and a deep understanding of FIRST values to everything we do. FTC represents a new chapter for us – one where we can push the boundaries of our engineering capabilities, work with more advanced technologies, and tackle increasingly complex challenges. But at our core, we remain the same team that fell in love with robotics in FLL: curious, collaborative, and committed to making a positive impact.

What Drives Us

We're not just building robots – we're building the future. Every project we undertake, every competition we enter, and every outreach effort we launch is driven by our desire to make engineering more accessible, more collaborative, and more impactful. From our base in Dublin, Ohio, we're working to strengthen the FIRST community that gave us so much. Whether we're mentoring younger teams, sharing our designs and code, or simply demonstrating that robotics can be both challenging and fun, we're committed to paying it forward and helping others discover their own passion for STEM.
